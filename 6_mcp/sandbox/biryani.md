## Chicken Fry Biryani Recipe

### Ingredients:
- 500g chicken pieces
- 2 cups basmati rice
- 1 cup yogurt
- 1 onion (chopped)
- 2 tomatoes (chopped)
- 1/2 cup cooking oil
- 1 cup water
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup fried onions
- 1/4 cup ghee
- 1/2 cup saffron strands (soaked in warm milk)
- Salt to taste
- Spices: cumin seeds, bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, green chilies

### Instructions:
1. **Marinate** chicken in yogurt, salt, and spices for 2 hours.
2. **Fry** marinated chicken in oil until golden. Set aside.
3. **Cook rice** in boiling water with saffron milk, drain.
4. **Saut√©** onions and tomatoes, add fried chicken, fry for 5 mins.
5. **Layer** rice, chicken, fried onions, and ghee in a pot.
6. **Simmer** on low heat for 20 mins, then let rest 10 mins before serving.
