# Chicken Fry Biryani Recipe

### Ingredients:
- 500g chicken thighs
- 1 cup basmati rice
- 2 onions (chopped)
- 1 cup yogurt
- 1 cup water
- 2 green chilies (sliced)
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- Salt to taste
- Vegetable oil

### Instructions:
1. **Marinate**: Mix chicken with yogurt, turmeric, salt, and red chili powder. Let rest for 30 mins.
2. **Cook Rice**: Rinse rice, cook in water until al dente. Set aside.
3. **Fry Onions**: Sauté onions until golden. Set aside.
4. **Brown Chicken**: Pan-fry marinated chicken until browned.
5. **Layer Biryani**: In a pot, layer cooked rice, chicken, onions, and green chilies. Sprinkle garam masala and cumin.
6. **Bake**: Cover and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25-30 mins until fragrant.
7. **Serve**: Garnish with fried onions and lemon wedges.

Enjoy your Chicken Fry Biryani!
